Privacy Policy

We collect information to provide better services to our users. We collect information in the following ways:

  • Information you give us. For example, our services require you to sign up for an account. When you do, we’ll ask for personal information, like your name, email address, telephone number, etc.
  • Information we get from your use of our services. We collect information about the services that you use and how you use them, like when you visit a website that uses our advertising services or view and interact with our ads and content.

How We Use Information We Collect

We use the information we collect from all of our services to provide, maintain, protect and improve them, to develop new ones, and to protect Theracis and our users. We also use this information to offer you tailored content – like giving you more relevant search results and ads.

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, and individuals outside of Theracis unless one of the following circumstances applies:

    • Protect against harm to the rights, property, or safety of Theracis, our users, or the public as required or permitted by law.
